What are the three main design advantages that object orientation can provide? Explain each concept.
===============================================================================

The three main design advantages of OOP are:

- Encapsulation
- Abstraction
- Polymorphism

EEncapsulation refers to the fact that you keep the data about an object, and any logic about manipulating that data close to the object definition itself.
